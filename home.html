About Me

My name is Mckenzie Reid, I'm a home based certified eyelash technician living in Burnaby, British Columbia. I have not always been in the beauty industry, infact my previous job was far from it working in fire and rescue. I fell in love with eyelash extensions in 2019 and pursued my dream of working as an eyelash technician in Parksville British Columbia. I recently moved to Burnaby to expand to a larger clientel and pursue what I love which is making other people feel great about themselves. It's an extremely rewarding job everytime I see a client leave with a smile on their face. I hope to see you at my studio soon!
